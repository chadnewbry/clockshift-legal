Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a time zone transition date wrong?

Clock Shift uses your device's time zone database provided by Apple. Make sure your device is running the latest iOS version for the most up-to-date data.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings in the App. Your purchases are tied to your Apple ID and should restore automatically. If not, try signing out and back into the App Store.

How do I manage notifications?

You can configure DST reminders in the App's Settings screen, or manage notification permissions in iOS Settings → Clock Shift → Notifications.
