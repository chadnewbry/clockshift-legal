Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Clock Shift ("the App") is developed and operated by Chad Newbry, LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Clock Shift does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All data processing happens entirely on your device or through Apple's services.

  • No accounts or sign-in required.
  • No analytics or tracking. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs.

Device Permissions

Clock Shift may request access to the following device capabilities. Each is optional and only used when you enable the related feature:

  • Location (When In Use): Used to determine your local time zone and DST rules. Your location is processed on-device and never transmitted to any server.
  • Apple Health (HealthKit): Clock Shift can read your sleep data to create a personalized sleep adjustment plan, and optionally write bedtime suggestions. Health data is processed entirely on-device and is never accessible to us.
  • Calendar: Clock Shift can read your calendar to alert you about meetings that may be affected by upcoming DST transitions. Calendar data is processed on-device only.
  • Notifications: Used to send reminders before DST transitions. Notification scheduling is handled locally on your device.
  • Siri: Clock Shift supports Siri Shortcuts so you can quickly ask when clocks change next.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your preferences (such as selected time zones, notification settings, manual clock checklists, and team members) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your app data (preferences, saved locations, and team members) is synced across your devices using Apple's iCloud and CloudKit infrastructure. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Clock Shift does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
