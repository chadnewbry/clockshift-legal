Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Clock Shift ("the App") is developed and operated by Chad Newbry, LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Clock Shift does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device.

  • No accounts or sign-in required.
  • No analytics or tracking. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No location data. Time zone information is read from your device settings and never leaves your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store your preferences (such as selected time zones and notification settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Clock Shift does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. If you make an in-app purchase, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
